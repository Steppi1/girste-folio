Stefano Girard
me@girste.com
I'm a designer from Italy, living in the mountains near Turin. My journey began at age 6 with playful collages in MS Word, where Clippy once asked what I'd like to do, and I answered, "this, for life." I’ve been hooked ever since. I craft visual identities for brands, driven by creativity and fresh ideas. Alongside design, I’m passionate about writing and often take on copywriting projects. I love collaborating with clients, sharing the process to help them achieve their vision. With agency experience behind me, I now work independently and dive into personal projects with fellow creatives. Feel free to reach out for work, collaborations, or just to say hi! ♥ ♥
